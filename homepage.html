Hi! I'm Faye

A photography student with a heart full of pixels and a camera full of stories.

You know that feeling when you see a photo that gives you chills? That's what I live for.

Being in front of the lens is fun, but being behind it? That's where the real adventure begins.
Every click is a chance to freeze time, play with light, and uncover beauty in the unexpected.
And let's be honest---without photos and videos, the internet would just be... well, a bunch of boring text. Yawn!

This website is my little corner to share what I've discovered so far.
Photography has taught me to see the world with wonder, and I'd love for you to see it that way too.
I may not have all the answers yet, but that’s the beauty of it: we’re all growing, one snapshot at a time.
So whether you’re a fellow beginner or just someone who appreciate a good photo, let’s explore this beautiful, pixel-filled journey together.
After all, the best shots are the ones we take while figuring things out!
